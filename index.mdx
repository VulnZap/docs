## How It Works

Install, connect, and scan in under five minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install">
    `npx vulnzap init` or `npm i -g vulnzap`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate">
    Authenticate once, pick the repo or workspace, enable inline detection
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan and fix">
    Findings stream to editor, CLI, and CI with ready-to-merge patches
  </Step>
  <Step title="Policies">
    Toggle SOC 2-aligned policies, logging, and egress controls when needed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Merge clean diffs and keep CI fast
  </Step>
</Steps>
