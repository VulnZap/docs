## Billing

### Payment Methods

- **Credit Card**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **ACH** (Enterprise only)
- **Wire Transfer** (Enterprise only)
- **Purchase Order** (Enterprise only)

### Billing Cycles

- **Monthly**: Pay month-to-month, cancel anytime
- **Annual**: Save 20% with annual billing

**Annual Pricing:**
- Standard: $182/seat/year (save $46)
- Scale: $758/seat/year (save $190)

### Adding/Removing Seats

- **Add seats**: Prorated charge immediately
- **Remove seats**: Effective next billing cycle
- **No penalties**: Cancel or downgrade anytime

### Usage-Based Billing

Overages on line scans (if you exceed plan limits):

- Standard: $0.10 per 1,000 additional lines
- Scale: $0.05 per 1,000 additional lines
- Enterprise: Unlimited (no overages)

<Info>
  You'll receive warnings at 80% and 95% usage to prevent unexpected charges.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do unused scans roll over to next month?">
    No, scan quotas reset each billing period. However, cached scans don't count against your quota.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my scan limit?">
    Free plan: Scanning pauses until next month or you upgrade.
    
    Paid plans: Automatic overages charged at usage-based rates (you'll get warnings first).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade mid-cycle?">
    Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated charges. Downgrades take effect at next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do you count seats?">
    One seat = one user with access to VulnZap. Seats are shared across your organization, not per-project.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What counts as a 'line scan'?">
    Each line of code analyzed counts as one line scan. Only changed lines are counted in incremental scans.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    We offer prorated refunds within 30 days of purchase if you're not satisfied.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I pay annually?">
    Yes! Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly billing.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a startup discount?">
    Yes! Contact sales@vulnzap.com with details about your startup for custom pricing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
